= Chapter 11: AI in Institutions

This chapter covers governance: policies and constraints, cost control and rate limiting, responsible AI (fairness, bias, transparency), A/B testing, GPU-backed evaluation, epistemology and infrastructure, and the governance tool. The governance tool—define_policy, check_compliance, simulate, report—enables the agent to check itself. It integrates into student-ai/ and completes the institutional layer. Labs 1–3 build the governance simulator with policies, cost tracking, bias audit, A/B testing, and epistemic scenarios. By chapter end, the agent operates under governance. Ch12 deploys the governed capstone.

==== Learning Objectives
After this chapter you will be able to:
* Define governance policies, constraints, and compliance checks
* Implement cost control, rate limiting, and responsible AI practices
* Conduct A/B testing and bias audit
* Build the governance tool (define_policy, check_compliance, simulate, report)

==== Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs)
By the end of this course, you will be able to:
* Define governance policies, constraints, and compliance checks (PLO 4).
* Implement cost control, rate limiting, and responsible AI practices (PLO 4).
* Conduct A/B testing and bias audit (PLO 3).
