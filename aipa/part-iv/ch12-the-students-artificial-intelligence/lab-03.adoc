==== Steps

. Run full evaluation: benchmark tasks from your domain. Collect user feedback (2–3 users). Document strengths, limitations, failure modes.
. Draft thesis: problem, approach, implementation, evaluation, discussion. Iterate with feedback.
. Prepare presentation: rehearse demo (live or recorded). Structure: intro, problem, approach, demo, conclusion, Q&A.
. Run demo: complete 2–3 representative tasks. Record or document for submission.
. Submit: complete student-ai repository, thesis (or summary), demo video or writeup, final submission to GitHub Classroom.
. Deploy for real use if applicable. Iterate based on feedback.
