# AIPA 502 — AI in Practice

## Credits and contact

- **Credits:** 3
- **Lecture:** 8 sessions × 90 minutes = 12 contact hours
- **Lab:** 12–24 hours (define per institution)

## Prerequisites

- Program entry: see [MSc_PROGRAM_SPEC.md](../MSc_PROGRAM_SPEC.md).
- **Chapter-specific:** Ch1 (Intelligence as Process).

## Course learning outcomes (CLOs)

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. Describe deployment, data pipelines, model lifecycle, and observability for AI systems (PLO 4).
2. Identify failure modes, brittleness, and human-AI collaboration patterns (PLO 4).
3. Build and integrate an audit tool (inventory, trace schema, pipeline) (PLO 2).
